Moorland Suite

Life at Moorland Suite

Our Moorland Suite is dedicated to providing a male only environment. There are 28 bedrooms all of which have accessible ensuite bathrooms. There are large communal spaces which all have exquisite views of the majestic Peak District. The Moorland suite is unique and offers targeted support, care and activities suited for male residents. The team members are by and large male staff members.

We provide a hydration station that encourages residents to drink whenever they wish and an opportunity to enjoy a variety of snacks. To ensure that our residents are supported in these communal spaces, there is a nursing station where trained staff are ready to assist should the need arise.


The suite has its own entrance with good access to parking for family, friends and those involved in the resident’s care. Bedrooms and day spaces boast scenic views of the stunning peak district and there are several larger rooms. For those of our residents who enjoy spending time outside, we have spacious private gardens which offer the opportunity to embrace the outdoors.

Community Spaces

Designed for interaction and socialising. The suite has a large, spacious, and bright lounge decorated in dementia-aware tones, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. Includes a well-stocked kitchenette for access to food throughout the day.

Bedrooms

Carefully furnished ensuite bedrooms provide a safe and personal space. We encourage residents to personalize their rooms with possessions and mementos. Each room is fitted with technological aids like nurse call systems and sensor mats.

Gardens

Large, private, and secure gardens featuring mature trees, shrubs, and flowers. Residents can engage in gardening or enjoy the fresh air from seating areas overlooking the calming beauty of the Peak District.

Key Features

No Hidden Costs

Dedicated Team

Fresh Environment

Situated in the Peak District, offering a clean, healthy, and scenic living space.
