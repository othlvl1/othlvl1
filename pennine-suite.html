The Pennine Suite

Adapting and living with dignity

Experience The Pennine Suite

The Pennine Suite is a mixed gender space that has 30 beautifully and sensitively decorated bedrooms. Residents and their families have a canvas on which to create a room that is individual to them, with ample space for pictures and treasured mementos.

Bedrooms and day spaces boast scenic views of the stunning Peak District. There are several larger rooms with ensuite bathrooms and comfortable homely meeting rooms for quality time with loved ones.

Contact Us

Community

We recognise the importance of community. Our large lounge area allows residents to socialise, engage in activities, or simply gaze out of picture windows at the Peak District landscapes.

The Detail: Spacious dining rooms create a friendly atmosphere with clustered tables for communal eating experiences.

Bedrooms

Spacious, well-appointed rooms that we encourage residents to personalise. Many feature ensuite bathrooms and all offer breathtaking views of the surrounding hills.

The Detail: Safety is paramount; each room includes nurse call systems, sensor mats, and bespoke technological aids.

Gardens

Our lush gardens offer mature trees, shrubs, and flowers. Well-placed seating allows residents to enjoy nature in a peaceful, secure environment.

The Detail: During summer, we host organised outdoor activities to maximize the benefits of fresh air and social interaction.
